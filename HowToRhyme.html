How to Rhyme with Colours and Shapes (2020):
Day and night variations of a childrens concertina aimed at creating a more playful and interactive experience for learning how to rhyme. The two variations aim to create different experiences for the children, as each colour palette communicates a very distinct feeling whilst reading.






10x10cm printed on 250gsm Freelife Felt paperstocks.
