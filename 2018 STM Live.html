Stop The Mets: STM Live

Stop The Mets is Australia's only registered fundraiser exclusively focused on raising awareness and funds for Metastatic Cancer Research.

The 2018 Stop the Mets LIVE music event was held at Hightail Bar Melbourne and proudly supported by Triple R. The event featured live music by Big Words, Lakyn, Maya Hirasedo, Peter Sonic, Sam Boon and friends, Wax Jax and the Midnight Snax and DJ sets by Autosea and Finding Figaro. By purchasing tickets, our guests continue to support the research undertaken by Professor John T Price and Joe Polidano, for the development of new anti-metastatic cancer treatments.

We used the illustration from the 2018 Naif Melbourne release via the clients request. The illustration was used for advertising and for t-shirt prints.
