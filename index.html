1995-1997

As our full name,
MUDLARK:
AN ELECTRONIC JOURNAL
OF POETRY & POETICS,
suggests, we will consider
accomplished work that
locates itself anywhere
on the spectrum of
contemporary practice.
We want poems, of course,
but we want essays, too,
that make us read poems
(and write them?)
differently somehow.
Although we are
not innocent, we do
imagine ourselves
capable of surprise.
