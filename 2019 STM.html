Stop The Mets: Melbourne Marathon Festival illustration

Stop The Mets is Australia's only registered fundraiser exclusively focused on raising awareness and funds for Metastatic Cancer Research.

Stop the Mets' supporters donned their runners to participate in the Melbourne Marathon Festival on Sunday, October 13th. As a result of the efforts, goodwill and generosity of everyday people; our team, their supporters and our major sponsors, Stop the Mets donated $16,577 to our beneficiary, Victoria University (Philanthropic Fund, formerly Victoria University Foundation).
