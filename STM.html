Stop The Mets
Stop the Mets is Australia's only registered fundraiser exclusively focused on raising awareness and funds for Metastatic Cancer Research. 100% of profits raised support the research team (Cancer Biology and Metastasis Research Group) of Professor John T Price and Joe Polidano, for the development of new anti-metastatic cancer treatments.
[1] STM LIVE (2018): The 2018 Stop the Mets LIVE music event was held at Hightail Bar Melbourne and proudly supported by Triple R.

[2] Stop the Mets @ Melbourne Marathon (2019): Stop the Mets' supporters donned their runners to participate in the Melbourne Marathon Festival.

[3] Recycle for Research (2020): Electing, selling and giving new life to one item that no longer 'sparks joy' and kindly donating the funds to STM.
