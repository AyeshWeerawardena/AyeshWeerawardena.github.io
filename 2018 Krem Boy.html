Krem Boy 'OEUF' Tee (first release)

Krem Boy is a personal project where I can test/challenge ideas and translate them into apparel. Although not a primary goal, I've always found it important to try new things in order to find what I like. The Oeuf tee speaks words about the love and joy I want my work to emanate, and making things doesn't always have to be taken too seriosly.
