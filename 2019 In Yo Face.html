'In Yo Face' Exibition, in collaboration with Oliver King and Angus Brown

Monash University student run exhibition space allowed us to exhibit our curated concept revolving around the idea that 'it's all figurative.' the three of us worked on collabourative works as well as our own to create a vibrant, interractive space for all to play in. The exhibition ran for 3 days on in the fine art department on Monash Caulfield campus.

[vid 1] 'Untitled': a collaborative, multi-media visual that was projected on a large wall during the exhibition.
[img 1+2] 'Cops n' Robs' & 'K.O!': two comissioned illustrations done with ink on A2 paper.
[img 3] 'Untitled': 3 individual stretchers with acrylic on canvas. the unprimed canvas aims to relate to the raw, simple nature of the whole composition.
[img 4]'Untitled': An interractive piece that allowed viewers to draw their own character on the pink acrylic blobs displayed.
