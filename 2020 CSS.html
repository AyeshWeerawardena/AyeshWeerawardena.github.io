CSS_303 FishEye EP Cover Art and Supporting Illustration

Michael Guiese's, aka. CSS_303, first EP release of 2020, featuring 4 tracks. Michael's' brief was to create a supporting character that matched the sound of the EP. The EP was released on Soundcloud and Bandcamp.
